About Mirza Resort

Nestled along a secluded stretch of coastline, Mirza Resort offers an exclusive sanctuary for discerning travelers seeking tranquility and luxury. Our resort blends contemporary elegance with traditional island charm.

Since 2005, we've been providing guests with unforgettable experiences, from our award-winning spa to our gourmet dining options. Our commitment to sustainability and exceptional service ensures that your stay is both luxurious and responsible.

  • 120 Luxury Suites & Villas
  • Private Beach Access
  • World-Class Spa & Wellness Center
  • Three Gourmet Restaurants
  • 24/7 Concierge Service
  • Eco-Friendly Practices
